Target Audience

The target audience for this website are people who need a quick update for their weather. They don't necessarily have a lot of time to stop and browse, so they visit our site to get their essentials for the forecast. The audience consist of busy or working people who may be on the move with ages ranging from 20 - 50 years old. Audience will typically use a mobile device.
